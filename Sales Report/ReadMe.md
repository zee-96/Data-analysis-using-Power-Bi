## üìä Dashboard Insights & Components

The dashboard is structured to answer key business questions across three levels: time, category, and geography.

| Component | Visualization Type | Analytical Insight |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Profit and Sales by Year** | **Dual-Axis Line Chart** | Tracks **annual growth and trends** from 2011 to 2014, confirming consistent revenue expansion. |
| **Total Sales by Category** | **Stacked Bar Chart** | Visualizes sales segmented by **Product Category** (Technology, Office Supplies, Furniture) and layered by **Customer Segment** (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office). |
| **Profit by Country** | **Interactive Map Visual** | Highlights **country-wise profit distribution**, allowing stakeholders to focus immediately on high-margin or underperforming regions in Europe. |
| **Highest Profitable Products** | **Treemap** | Focuses attention on the **Top 5 Products** by profit margin, identifying the most valuable items in the inventory. |
